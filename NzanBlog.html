Making dreams come to life

Dream I couldn't ignore

  • Admin
  • June 23, 2023

Friendship and business can often be a delicate balance. When my good friend Alongkum invited me to join his startup, I found myself at a crossroads, torn between the familiarity of our friendship and the exciting potential of a new professional adventure. The decision to join a startup can be both thrilling and nerve-racking. But for me this journey was an exciting leap into the unknown. In this blog, I'll share my journey of how I came to join Alongkum's startup and the lessons I learned along the way.

The Genesis of the Idea:
My journey with Alongkum's startup began with a casual conversation over a coffee shop. We talked about our dreams and ambitions, and it was during one of these conversations that he shared his vision for a new venture. It was an idea that resonated with me and piqued my interest immediately.

The Invitation:
On one faithful afternoon Alongkum formally invited me to join his startup. The proposition was both exciting and daunting. On one hand, it was an opportunity to work alongside someone I trust. On the other, it meant stepping into the unknown and taking on the responsibilities of a startup venture.

The Decision-Making Process:
The decision to join a friend's startup is not one to be taken lightly. I had to consider various factors:
Alignment of Goals: I evaluated whether our professional goals were in sync. Did I share the same vision and passion for the startup's mission?
Trust and Compatibility: Trust is a vital element in any business partnership. My existing friendship with Alongkum gave us a head start in this regard, but we still needed to ensure our working styles and values aligned.
Skills and Contributions: I assessed how my skills and experience would complement the team. It was important for me to contribute meaningfully to the venture.
Potential Challenges: We also discussed potential challenges that might arise from combining friendship and business, such as how we would handle disagreements or conflicts.

The leap of Faith:
After careful consideration and thorough discussions, I decided to take the leap and join Alongkum's startup. It was a leap of faith, driven by our shared passion for the venture and our belief in each other's abilities. I understood that challenges would arise, but I was confident that our strong foundation of friendship would help us navigate them.

The Thrill of Milestones:
Despite the challenges, achieving milestones became incredibly rewarding. From launching our first ever official website to building our first major project, each success fueled our determination to keep pushing forward. The thrill of hitting milestones and witnessing the growth of the startup together has been immensely rewarding. Celebrating these achievements together made the journey even more special.

The Road ahead:
Joining Techicynics has been a remarkable journey. It's a testament to the power of shared dreams, trust, and the potential that exists in combining friendship with entrepreneurship. While challenges are inevitable, they are opportunities for growth and learning. As I look ahead, I'm excited about the future of our startup and grateful for the chance to be part of something special.
