Building Dreams Together and the Unveiling Journey of TechiCynics

  • Admin
  • June 23, 2023

Every entrepreneurial journey begins with a dream, an idea that sparks passion and the determination to make a difference. In the vast ocean of startups, one company has emerged with a vision that has the potential to disrupt an entire industry. This blog aims to shed light on TechiCynics, an innovative venture, and the incredible team behind it that is driving its success.

The Birth of TechiCynics: TechiCynics was born out of a shared vision to revolutionize the IT industry. Founded by Alongkum, Co-founded by Moalong and Andrew Ozukum in 2021, this visionary startup has set out on a mission to approach and achieve different everyday problems solution in the field of service solutions, software development, mobile development, web development, IOT and many more

A Startup is Born: Fuelled by my passion, I decided to turn my ideas into reality by co-founding a startup. The process was both challenging and rewarding, as I navigated through the intricacies of market research, business planning, and assembling a talented team. Together, we worked tirelessly to transform our vision into a tangible product or service.

The Passionate Founder: Alongkum,Moalong and Andrew Ozukum, the driving force behind TechiCynics, is a visionary with a deep-rooted passion for innovation. They possess an extraordinary blend of industry expertise, creativity, and leadership skills. Winners of innovation trigger under start up Nagaland program which was organized by National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology Kohima funded by department of Industries and Commerce Government of Nagaland.

The Dynamic Team: Behind every successful startup is a team of individuals who complement each other's strengths and work synergistically towards a common goal. The team at TechiCynics is no exception. Let's meet some of the remarkable individuals who make up this dynamic team:

Founder and CEO:- Alongkum, UI/UX Designer, Android Development and IoT

Co-Founder and CTO:- Nzanthung Odyuo, Web Development, IoT and Artificial Intelligence.

Chief Developer:- Bipothung Humtsoe, Full Stack Web Development.

Developer:- Anup Dey, App Development.

These are just a few examples of the exceptional individuals who form the backbone of Techicynics. Their collective expertise, passion, and dedication to the company's vision have created a powerhouse of talent that fuels innovation and propels the company forward.

Culture of Collaboration and Innovation: At TechiCynics, the team operates in an environment that fosters collaboration, creativity, and innovation. The startup values diverse perspectives and encourages open communication, allowing ideas to flow freely and be refined collectively. This culture of collaboration not only brings out the best in each team member but also helps TechiCynics stay at the forefront of industry trends and advancements.

The story of TechiCynics and its exceptional team is one of ambition, determination, and the relentless pursuit of innovation. With a passionate founder leading the way and a team of remarkable individuals working together, the startup is poised for remarkable success. As TechiCynics continues to disrupt the industry and make a positive impact, the world eagerly awaits the groundbreaking solutions.
