Winners of Start Up Nagaland Innovation trigger

  • Admin
  • June 23, 2023

We have won the Startup Nagaland which was organized by National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology Kohima funded by Department of Industries and commerce Government of Nagaland.PARQUE is a project based on hardware and software and the interface will be an Android app. PARQUE will minimize the time spent by drivers looking for parking spaces, it will also reduce traffic congestion and avoid improper parking.With Parque you can look for vacant spot for parking around the city with the help of your smart phone without having to look around the city. Our goal is to solve the current parking problems in our system and reduce human resources.

Another Achievement by the team 2nd runner up of “Startup Nagaland” organized by National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology Kohima funded by Department of Industries and Commerce Government of Nagaland. With the project Aoli Penzur
